About

This site makes it easy to visualize metes and bounds legal descriptions on a map. All you have to do is enter the legal description in the box below, and it will try to draw that legal description if possible.

Note: This site has no guarantee of accuracy and should not be relied on for legal work. If you require help with property law, please hire a lawyer. If you are in Illinois, my law firm of Allen & Korkowski & Associates would love to help you with any property law questions.
